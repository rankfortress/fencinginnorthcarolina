High Point is a city located in the Piedmont Triad region of North Carolina. High Point is North Carolina's only city that extends into four counties. The city has a total population over 110,000. High Point is currently the ninth-largest municipality in North Carolina, and the 259th-largest city in America.Major industries in High Point include furniture, textiles, and bus manufacturing.
